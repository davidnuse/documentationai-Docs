## Overview

Page metadata helps search engines understand your content.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add title and description">
    ````html
    <title>Home - My Website</title>
    <meta name="description" content="Welcome to my awesome website." />
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check implementation">
    Reload your page and view source to confirm tags are present.
  </Step>
</Steps>
