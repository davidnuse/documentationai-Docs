## Overview

SEO stands for Search Engine Optimization. It helps your website rank higher in search results like Google.

## Why is SEO Important?

- Increases visibility
- Drives organic traffic
- Improves user experience

## Basic Components

- Titles and meta descriptions
- Structured content
- Quality backlinks

Improve these areas for higher rankings.
