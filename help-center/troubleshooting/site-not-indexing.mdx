---
title: Site Not Indexing
description: Fix issues when your site is not showing in search engines.
---

## Symptoms

- Your site does not appear in Google search
- "No results found" for your domain

## Causes

- Noindex tag on pages
- Blocked robots.txt
- Site recently launched

## Solution

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check robots.txt">
    ````bash
    # Ensure you don't see: Disallow: /
    curl https://yourdomain.com/robots.txt
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove noindex tags">
    Search your HTML for `<meta name="robots" content="noindex">` and remove it.
  </Step>
</Steps>
